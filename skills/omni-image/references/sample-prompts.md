# Sample Prompts

Curated prompt examples organized by category. Use these as starting points, then customize. Each prompt follows the scaffold pattern: subject, style, composition, lighting, color, background, constraints.

## Product Photography

**Clean product shot**
> A single bottle of artisanal hot sauce centered on a white marble surface. Hyper-real product photography. Straight-on angle, centered framing with generous negative space. Soft diffused studio lighting from above with a subtle rim light. Warm amber tones against cool white. Clean white background with soft shadow beneath. No text, no labels visible.

**Lifestyle context**
> A ceramic pour-over coffee dripper mid-pour, dark coffee streaming into a handmade mug. Lifestyle product photography. Three-quarter angle, shallow depth of field on the stream. Warm morning window light from the left. Earth tones: cream, brown, soft green from a potted plant. Kitchen counter with a linen napkin and a bag of coffee beans. No branding.

## Food & Beverage

**Cocktail**
> A coupe glass filled with a vibrant violet butterfly pea flower cocktail, a single ice sphere with shifting blue-to-purple tones. Vivid hyper-real photography. Slightly low angle, centered subject. Warm backlight through the glass with cool blue fill light from the front. Jewel tones: violet, deep blue, gold rim reflection. Dark bar counter with bokeh Edison bulbs in background.

**Plated dish**
> A perfectly plated risotto with seared scallops, microgreens, and a drizzle of saffron oil on a matte black plate. Fine dining food photography. Overhead flat lay, asymmetric placement. Soft directional light from 10 o'clock position. Warm golds and creamy whites against the dark plate. Dark slate table with a folded linen napkin at frame edge.

## Architecture & Interiors

**Exterior**
> A mid-century modern desert house with floor-to-ceiling glass walls and a flat roof, set against red sandstone cliffs. Architectural photography. Wide angle, eye level, building at one-third. Late afternoon golden hour with long shadows across the sand. Warm terracotta, sage green landscaping, pale sky. Sonoran desert with saguaro cacti.

**Interior**
> A minimalist Japanese-inspired living room with tatami mats, a low walnut table, and a single ikebana arrangement. Interior design photography. Wide angle from doorway, leading lines along the floor. Soft natural light from shoji screens on the left wall. Neutral palette: warm wood, ivory, charcoal accents. No clutter, no electronics visible.

## Portraits & People

**Environmental portrait**
> A ceramicist at their wheel, hands covered in wet clay, focused expression. Environmental portrait photography. Medium shot, shallow depth of field with the kiln soft in the background. Warm directional light from a high window. Earthy tones: terracotta, cream apron, grey studio walls. Workshop setting with shelves of drying pottery.

**Stylized portrait**
> A woman in 1920s flapper attire, finger waves, pearl earrings, looking over her shoulder. Vintage Hollywood glamour portrait. Tight crop from shoulders up, slight tilt. Rembrandt lighting with a strong key light from the right. High contrast black and white with rich midtones. Dark textured studio backdrop.

## Illustration & Art

**Children's book**
> A small fox in a knitted scarf walking through an autumn forest, leaves falling around it. Soft watercolor children's book illustration. Central figure, eye level, plenty of surrounding space. Warm diffused light filtering through the canopy. Autumnal palette: burnt orange, golden yellow, forest green, cream. Gentle, whimsical mood.

**Technical illustration**
> An exploded view diagram of a mechanical pocket watch showing all gears, springs, and the escapement mechanism. Technical illustration with clean linework. Orthographic exploded view, components spread vertically. Even flat lighting with no shadows. Monochrome brass-gold on white background. Labels optional, components clearly separated.

## Textures & Backgrounds

**Surface texture**
> A closeup of hand-hammered copper with a verdigris patina, showing tool marks and oxidation variations. Macro texture photography. Straight overhead, filling the entire frame. Soft even lighting to eliminate harsh reflections. Teal-green verdigris against warm copper. Seamless, suitable for tiling as a background.

**Atmospheric background**
> Subtle dark textured background with faint Art Deco geometric patterns in gold, suitable as a menu or poster backdrop. Graphic design texture. Flat, no perspective, edge-to-edge coverage. Minimal lighting variation, slightly brighter center for vignette. Near-black with warm gold accents at 20% opacity. No focal point, no subjects.

## Icons & UI

**App icon**
> A simple, bold icon of a compass rose with four cardinal points, thick lines, rounded corners. Flat design app icon. Centered, square framing with padding. No shadows, no gradients, flat color only. Deep navy blue on white. Transparent background, no text.

**UI illustration**
> A friendly illustrated empty-state graphic of an astronaut floating in space, waving, with tiny stars around. Flat vector illustration style. Centered figure, plenty of whitespace. No directional lighting, flat color fills. Soft pastel palette: lavender, mint, peach, white. Transparent background.

## Concept Art

**Environment**
> A floating sky city connected by rope bridges, with waterfalls pouring off the edges into clouds below. Fantasy concept art. Wide establishing shot, looking slightly upward. Dramatic crepuscular rays through cloud gaps. Cool blues and warm oranges where light hits the stone. Expansive sky with layered cumulus clouds.

**Character**
> A desert nomad in layered linen robes and a sand-mask, carrying a staff with a glowing crystal tip. Character concept art, full body turnaround reference sheet. Three views: front, side, back on the same canvas. Flat even lighting for reference clarity. Warm sand and ochre palette with a teal crystal glow. Plain grey background.
